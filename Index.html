Welcome to ABC Brickworks!

Well, at least to an interactive map of it. I'm Henry Dominguez-Letelier, a pure-bred nerd, foodie, startupista, and student at Yale-NUS. I've made this map as part of my final project for a class called Anthropology of Food, but I promise no intense research here, just an interactive dive into ABC Brickworks.

Please click the red buttons to start exploring around, I'd recommend starting with ABC then hopping around inside the center following the A1 - C4 order. After that feel free to check out the circles around the center which feature exits and some public service announcement art. As a map key, the blue and green boxes are non-halal and halal tray return points respectivley and the zones were created based on stalls directly toucing / facing eachother.

I hope you enjoy and please do press all the red buttons!
